    Discussion: Meet your classmates

    Introduce Yourself and Reflect on Participation

    This first discussion is an opportunity for you to reflect and share your thoughts on the importance of social interaction and participation with others in your daily life and this online course.

    Create a reply to this post using the following instructions as a guide.

    TIP: First, write your reply on a separate document (not directly in the discussion area) and upload any images you want to include in your post to the Files area of the course. Review How to embed an image in a discussion post. Then, copy and paste the text and insert your image into the discussion form. Why do this? Just in case there are technical difficulties (like your computer freezes!) you have a copy of your answer elsewhere for quick retrieval!

    What to include:

    1. Briefly describe your personal interests and goals for your education and in this course in particular. Optional: include a photo of yourself, your family, friend or pet, or where you live.
    2. Participation is an important aspect in online learning. In 1-2 sentences describe an example in your own life where social interaction and participation are important.

    Respond to at least two of your peers with further inquiry about their interest and social interaction examples. For example, do you relate to their interests and experiences and in what way? Perhaps you want to learn more about  something they expressed?  You may respond either in writing or utilize the Audio/Video Messages option within Canvas. Review this tutorial on how to use the Audio/Video Messages option.
